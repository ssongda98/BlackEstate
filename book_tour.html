Black Estate Wine Tour

We greet you in our Netherwood Vineyard, share our approach to organic & biodynamic farming and viticulture and show you around our traditional winery. Your tour finishes in our Winery Room with a seated tasting of six wines accompanied by snacks.

For tours at 11am each day, possibly with other guests, we charge $95 per person. This tour is complimentary for club members, once a year (including 3 friends). Allow 45 minutes to 1.5 hours for the tour. Please book our Wine Tour online with the form below. The Wine Tour is intimate so we regret that we cannot accommodate groups larger than 8 people.

We can organise a private Wine Tour with either Nicholas or Penelope or one of their managers for $150 per person. A designated Winemaker Tour with Nicholas can also be booked on request – please contact us direct to make a time and confirm our fee at bookings@blackestate.co.nz.

The Winery Room for a private lunch

We can host larger groups for a private lunch in our Winery Room, taking in the views of Netherwood Vineyard and enjoying our wines, food and hospitality. Lunch is served in a simple sharing style and focuses on fresh organic and local produce. Enquire about The Winery Room for your private lunch (and tasting)

Hold your group’s meeting at the Winery Room
We also invite businesses, families and groups to hire the Winery Room for private all day use for meetings or workshops. Connect to nature away from the hustle and have Netherwood Vineyard to yourselves. We can bring you lunch and offer the Wine Tour to compliment your day. Enquire here for Winery Room hire.
