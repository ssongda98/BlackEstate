Treble Rose 2020

Our three organic vineyards, together as one. We harvest 5 varieties across these vineyards in a 14 day period and progressively ferment the whole lot. Deep ruby. Rose hip, cranberry, strawberry, cardamon aroma. Bright mouth filling palate, salivating invigorating flavour length, wild berry liquorice with a hint of ripe green apple. Unfined and unfiltered. Serve chilled. Drink now to 2023.
