Black Estate B&B

A beautiful B & B at Black Estate’s organic Home vineyard, located on the ground floor of our architecturally designed building.
Our cellar door and lunch time restaurant are in the opposite end of this building. The views are vast, expanding from the garden and vineyard to the majestic Southern Alps. You can stay any night of the week but please note our restaurant and cellar door is closed Tuesday-Wednesday. On days that it is open, our restaurant closes at 4pm so you’ll have the place & sunset to yourselves. This is real life on the vineyard, where you may observe our staff in the vineyard (and the odd tractor), and the environment around our vines – the stars, resident rabbits and insects, plants and of course vines. The B & B is on the Waipara Valley Vineyard Trail and mountain bikes are provided for use while you are staying.
