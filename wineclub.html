Wine Club

The Haast’s Eagle membership

Sign up for 2 cases delivered in May and November and receive:

  • 15% off your two club cases and all other wine purchases all year (online or at our cellar door).
  • One complimentary Wine Tour at our Netherwood Vineyard for up to 4 people, per year.
  • Select cellar and limited edition wines.
  • Complimentary freight for 6+ bottles in NZ.
  • A club card to identify your membership when you visit us.

The Omihi Panther membership

Sign up for 1 cases delivered in May and November and receive:

  • 10% of your one case club and all other wine purchases all year (online or at our cellar door).
  • One complimentary Wine Tour at our Winery & Netherwood Vineyard for up to 4 people, per year.
  • Select cellar and limited edition wines.
  • Complimentary freight for 6+ bottle cases in New Zealand.
  • A cute club card to identify your membership when you visit us.

All we ask

By joining The Club you agree:

  • To purchase a minimum of 12 bottles per year.
  • Unless cancelled in writing, your annual subscription to the Club will automatically renew on the anniversary of the date below.
  • If you cancel your membership or amend your scheduled order after it has been dispatched, you will be charged the cost of collecting and/or redirecting.
  • Complimentary freight in New Zealand for 6+ bottles.
  • If we don’t hear back from you when confirming your delivery, we will send the suggested mix of wines to your last notified address you agreed to take the wine.
  • You are 18 years of age or older.
